Hi All,

So, very sorry for the looong delay in posting my modifications to get 
sn to build on OSX.

Here they are:

1/ fixing library generation issues

The super-obsolete libtool that is included with libdb should be 
updated. The version included in db is 1.5.22; it does not know how to 
compile the library properly. I installed a fresher copy of libtool 
(2.2) and symlinked  db/PORT/libtool to it. Solved the library 
compilation issue.

2/ fixing linking issues:

db/Makefile
(-) dbimp_LDADD = -L. -ldbutils -L$(DB_LIBDIR) -ldb \
(+) dbimp_LDADD = -L. -ldbutils $(DB_LIBDIR)/libdb.a \


hyper/Makefile
(-) -L$(DBUtilsLibDir) -ldbutils -L$(DB_LIBDIR) -ldb \
(+) -L$(DBUtilsLibDir) -ldbutils $(DB_LIBDIR)/libdb.a \

I have no experience with autotools and I do not have time to study them 
at the moment. I hope one of you can transform my Makefile fixes into 
fixes to the automake configuration files.

Best,
Jacques
