Dear all,

version NG4.1 has just been released. Currently, it's only available
in the SVN repository.

SVN people can switch to (or checkout) the following URL:
https://svn.berlios.de/svnroot/repos/sourcenav/tags/SN-NG4.1

Tarballs and binary builds may follow in the next couple of days here:
http://developer.berlios.de/project/showfiles.php?group_id=8334

NG4.1 is a maintenance / bugfix release to the stable NG4 series.
It fixes a bug regarding the build dialog (clicking on a error message
should take you to the correct file now), and two nasty bugs on at
least Fedora ("no event type or button # or keysym" and some rendering
garbage).

As biggest bugfix - I mean, addition :) - comes quite a bit of php v5
support (thanks to Maurice).

Cheers and happy xref'ing,
Freek
