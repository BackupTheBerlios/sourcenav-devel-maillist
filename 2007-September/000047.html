Dear all,

a bug on at least x86_64 has been reported that is related to tcl's
rand() function; other 64bit architectures may be also affected.
It is also present in all previous source navigator versions (it's in
the tcl interpreter), but only manifests on NG1 and NG2. The splash
dialogue uses rand() to choose the startup image and might fail every
now and then, crashing SN.

This bug has been fixed in the latest trunk (thanks to Daniel).
If there is enough demand we might release an updated NG2.1 or provide
a simple patch (without the need for recompiling).

Cheers,
Freek
