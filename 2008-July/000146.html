If I'm not misunderstanding you:
Because SN doesn't really know. How should she (SN is a she, I'm sure
of it) know?
She doesn't know compile units and its corresponding programm
namespaces, and while she COULD know include hierarchies, she doesn't
really use them when generating Xrefs (IIRC).
All that counts is the symbol's NAME.
That's why the "there are more than one definition of the function -
please select" dialogue comes up from time to time.

>  If it did then b2(fu) would have a "referred by" reference. Instead there is this
