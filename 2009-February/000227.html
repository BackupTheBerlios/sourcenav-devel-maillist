This most probably is not the solution to your specific problem, but, on
Solaris 10 this was what I had to do:

in the sourcenavigator
folder:

perl -npi.bak -e 's/"cc"$/"gcc"/' db4/PORT/libtool perl -npi.
bak -e 's/hardcode_libdir_flag_spec\=.*$/hardcode_libdir_flag_spec=""
/'  db4/PORT/libtool
perl -npi.bak -e 's/\-lpthread/-lpthread -lrt/' 
snavigator/db/Makefile
perl -npi.bak -e 's/\-lpthread/-lpthread -lrt/' 
snavigator/hyper/Makefile
perl -npi.bak -e 's/\-lpthread/-lpthread - lrt/'
snavigator/parsers/cpp/Makefile
