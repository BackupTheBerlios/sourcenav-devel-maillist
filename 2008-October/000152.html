adopted in source navigator NG. As Freek indicates, SN should wait  
until xref has finished. In practice however, it doesn't always. On  
unix, it helped when we added a second of sleep. On windows it seems  
to behave differently  however. There, we check whether hyper.exe has  
finished running.
