Hi to all,

I used, now for many years, sourcenav 5.2b on Solaris 8 and 10.
As soon as I saw the new version I wanted to give it a try but I could 
not compile on Solaris 10.

The problem is in the configure script:
even if the real-time libraries (-lrt) are detected,
the switch -lrt is not actually added when compiling some of the 
components resulting into a link failure.

Adding manually the -lrt option to the gcc libs solves the problem.

Let me know if more details are needed.

Angelo
