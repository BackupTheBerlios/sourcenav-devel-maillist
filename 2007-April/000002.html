The < and > buttons do not work as expected immediately after Search
button is pressed.

The > button takes highlight to the last entry in list, not first.
Next press takes to first in list and then behaviour is as expected.

The < button takes highlight to the first entry in list, not last.
Further presses do nothing.  Subsequent > button presses also do
nothing until the number of presses of > match the number of presses
of < and then both buttons work as expected.

(It's always possible to get out of the 'funny' state by selecting an
entry in the list explicitly with the mouse).

Attached patch fixes problem.
Search > highlights first entry in list
Search < highlights last entry in list
