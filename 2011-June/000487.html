> I have just found the same error while compiling with *latest *MinGW. It
is cause by this statement

((Tcl_Obj **) objv) += (async + 3);

There's a good answer for it on
http://stackoverflow.com/questions/5879054/compiling-c-code-error-lvalue-required-as-left-operand-of-assignment

GCC 3.4.5's cast-as-lvalue language extension, which was deprecated in 3.4.5
and removed in 4.0, allows the result of a cast to be used as an lvalue if
the operand to the cast is an lvalue.

 I just happened to have an old MinGW with GCC 3.4.5 on my office
computer...
A workaround for this is

objv = ((Tcl_Obj **) objv) + (async + 3);

I suspect the other issues are also caused by the GCC version difference.
