Dear all,

Source Navigator NG4.4 has been released. It is the continuation of
the very stable NG4 series and took just about 4 months of
development.


Fixes:

- Fix dbcompact to deal with invalid DB files
- Display default cache size (8MB) in dbcompact's help output
- Rework autoconf stuff to work on win32 and Solaris again
- Add cmdline switch for db4 cache size to dbcompact
- Remove unneeded itcl/iwidgets3.0.0 from source


Enhancements:

- Add the possibility to selectively remove xref nodes in the xrefpane
(remove "Referred from" and "Refers to" nodes in graph)
Thanks to Medi for suggesting.


Obtaining SN NG:

SVN people can switch to (or checkout) the following URL:
https://svn.berlios.de/svnroot/repos/sourcenav/tags/SN-NG4.4

Tarballs and binary builds may follow in the next couple of days here:
http://developer.berlios.de/project/showfiles.php?group_id=8334

As always - we're still looking for help and volunteers.

Cheers,
Freek
